Gross profit margin is generally important because it is the starting point toward achieving a healthy net profit. When you have a high gross profit margin, you are in better position to have a strong operating profit margin and strong net income. This dashboard keeps track of the GPM and makes a Year Over Year comparison. Keep in mind that some products are not introduced to all market at the same time.
