Creating Profit Margin by Segment 10


Go to Charts and drag Combo chart to the sheet. Resize the object then change the title to ‘Profit Margin by Segment’.

Click add dimension and select Segment

Select GPM and YOY for the measures. Turn off the Master measure format then select number formatting with 2 decimal places. Repeat the same for YOY

Click on appearance and select presentation.

Select show Data Points and Turn on Point labels

Now go to Colors and legend (under appearance) and turn on colors but deselect show legend.
