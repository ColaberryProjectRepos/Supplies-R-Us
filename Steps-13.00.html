Creating Filter pane


Click on Charts then select filter pane under the chart options.

Click on the bottom right corner to add the filter pane to the sheet

Click on dimension to add Ship mode.

After adding the first dimension you will get another button to add dimension under the first one.

Click on it and add Year to the filter pane

Repeat the process to add Month to the filter pane




Sheet title styling

Start off by clicking on the ellipses before the project title

Select green (#318931) for the left side of the title background

Select red (#97260e) for the right side of the title background

Select Orange (#f8981d) for the font color

Then exit the box
