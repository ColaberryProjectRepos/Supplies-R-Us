Creating KPIs 9

Create KPI for Gross Profit Margin

Go to Charts and drag KPI to the sheet. Resize the object.

Click on Add Measure and Select Gross Profit Margin.

Click on the KPI and go to the right side of the screen.

Click on the dropdown next to GPM and turn off the Master Measure format

Click on Number under Number Formatting then pick the percentage with 2 decimal places.

Click on Appearance and select color. Turn off the conditional formatting and select dark grey


Create KPI for YOY

Go to Charts and drag KPI to the sheet. Resize the object.

Click on Add Measure and Select YOY and turn off the Master Measure format

Click on Number under Number Formatting then pick the percentage with 2 decimal places.

Click on Appearance and select color.

Turn on the conditional formatting. Add a limit and type 0. Pick red for left side with an icon and pick green for the right side with an icon
